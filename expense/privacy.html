Privacy Policy for Expense mnAI : Expense Tracker

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

At mnAI, we believe your financial data is exclusively yours. Our business model is based on providing a premium software experience through subscriptions, not by harvesting, analyzing, or selling your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how mnAI handles your data. The short version is: We do not collect, store, or have access to your financial data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

mnAI is built with a "Zero Developer Access" architecture. We do not have user accounts, we do not have our own servers, and we do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. Therefore, we do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Your transactions, account balances, or financial history.
  • Your custom categories, budgets, or bills.
  • Your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Your location or device identifiers for tracking purposes.

2. How Your Data is Stored and Synced

Local Storage: If you use the Free plan or have iCloud sync disabled, your financial data is stored locally in an encrypted database directly on your device.

iCloud Sync: If you use our premium sync features, your data is securely synchronized using Apple’s CloudKit infrastructure. Your data is stored in your personal, private iCloud database. We cannot see, access, or decrypt this data. Its transmission and storage are governed strictly by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

3. Shared Workspaces & Collaboration

If you use the Family Sharing plan to create Shared Workspaces, that specific workspace data is stored in a shared iCloud database. This allows the people you explicitly invite (via their Apple ID) to view and edit the data. You are solely responsible for who you invite to your Shared Workspaces. mnAI developers still have zero access to this shared data.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

All payments, subscriptions, and refunds are processed securely by Apple via the App Store. mnAI does not collect or have access to your credit card information, billing address, or payment details. We only receive an anonymous receipt from Apple confirming whether you have an active subscription so the app can unlock premium features.

5. Intelligence and Analytics

All "Intelligence" features, insights, and automated anomaly detections are processed strictly on-device using local algorithms. None of your data is sent to the cloud or any AI server for processing.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and notify you within the app.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at: mnAIapps@gmail.com
